GURKHA / THE STORY

01 / ORIGINS

SOME NAMES ARE CREATED. SOME NAMES ARE EARNED.

For generations, Gurkha soldiers have carried the highest honors in the British and Indian armies. Their courage, discipline, and mountain warfare mastery are the foundation of our premium eyewear.

02 / Lethal Perfection

The Iconic Blade

THE SHARPEST STATEMENT IN HISTORY

Armed with their signature forward-curving Kukri, the Gurkhas command respect globally. Our designs draw inspiration from the perfect balance and enduring sharpness of this legendary Nepalese blade.

03 / Absolute Precision

Immaculate Detail

CRAFTED FOR THE FEARLESS.

Soldiers from the Brigade of Gurkhas are renowned for their immaculate turnout and discipline. We mirror this exactitude, ensuring every frame meets the highest military-grade standards.

04 / Tactical Mastery

Flawless Logistics

PRECISION IN EVERY MOVE.

Members of the 94 Squadron Queen's Own Gurkha Logistic Regiment demonstrate that strength isn't just in combat, but in flawless execution. We apply this same precision to our craftsmanship.

Signature Series

Elite Clubmaster

POWER IN EVERY FRAME

Commanding attention with a distinctive browline architecture. The Elite Clubmaster fuses vintage intellectual authority with tactical-grade durability, featuring hand-polished acetate and shatter-resistant polarized optics.

Tactical Series

Rectangle Black

WHERE LEGEND MEETS LUXURY

Engineered for stealth and speed. The Tactical Rectangle boasts an aerodynamic, matte-black profile that grips securely during high-intensity movement, delivering razor-sharp peripheral clarity and uncompromising UV defense.

Heritage Series

Gold Aviator

UNMISTAKABLE IN STYLE

The definitive pilot's silhouette, forged in premium aerospace-grade alloy with subtle gold accents. Designed for high-altitude clarity, these teardrop lenses offer superior glare reduction and timeless military prestige.

Premium Collection

Tortoise Elite

FEARLESS CONFIDENCE

A bold statement of power and luxury. Hand-crafted from rich Havana tortoise acetate, the Elite series features reinforced 5-barrel hinges and amber-tinted polarized lenses that drastically enhance contrast and depth perception.

Features & Specs

Polarized Lenses

Advanced vertical polarization filter that blocks 99% of reflected glare, providing superior visual clarity and reducing eye strain in high-glare environments.

UV400 Protection

Comprehensive protection blocking 100% of harmful UVA and UVB rays, ensuring long-term optical health.

Anti-Reflective Coating

Rear-lens coating that prevents bounce-back glare, sharpening contrast and enhancing color perception.

Premium Materials

Pure Acetate Frames

Handcrafted from pure cellulose acetate, offering a rich, deep finish, hypoallergenic properties, and a lightweight, custom fit that adapts over time.

Medical-Grade Titanium
